🎁 GIFT CARDS - NEW SIMPLIFIED FLOW
====================================

✅ WHAT CHANGED:
- Removed all the complex options
- Now only 2 packages: Starter & Premium
- Simple 3-step process

📋 HOW IT WORKS:
================

STEP 1: Choose Package (gift-cards.html)
-----------------------------------------
Two options:
  🌟 Starter Package - KSh 2,500
     - Gift Card: KSh 2,500
     - Choose Greeting Card
     - Choose Flowers
     - Personal Message

  💎 Premium Package - KSh 5,000 (Most Popular)
     - Gift Card: KSh 5,000
     - Choose Greeting Card
     - Choose Premium Flowers
     - Personal Message
     - Gift Wrapping

Click on either package → Goes to cards-flowers.html

STEP 2: View Starter & Choose Items (cards-flowers.html)
---------------------------------------------------------
1. You see the starter package display showing:
   - Package name
   - Gift card value
   - "View Cards & Flowers" button

2. Click "View Cards & Flowers" button
   → Page scrolls down to show:
   
   GREETING CARDS (6 options):
   - Birthday Card - KSh 200
   - Congratulations Card - KSh 200
   - Thank You Card - KSh 200
   - Love Card - KSh 200
   - Anniversary Card - KSh 200
   - Blank Card - KSh 200
   
   Each card has 2 buttons:
   [Add to Cart] [Buy]

   FLOWERS (6 options):
   - Red Roses Bouquet - KSh 1,500
   - Tulips Bouquet - KSh 1,200
   - Mixed Bouquet - KSh 1,800
   - Luxury Arrangement - KSh 2,500
   - Sunflowers - KSh 1,000
   - Orchids - KSh 1,300
   
   Each flower has 2 buttons:
   [Add to Cart] [Buy]

3. Two ways to purchase:
   
   A) ADD TO CART:
      - Click "Add to Cart" on any card
      - Click "Add to Cart" on any flower
      - Cart appears in bottom-right corner
      - Shows all items + total
      - Click "Checkout" button in cart
   
   B) BUY NOW:
      - Click "Buy" on any single item
      - Goes directly to checkout with that item

STEP 3: Complete Purchase (gift-checkout.html)
-----------------------------------------------
1. See order summary with:
   - Package (Starter or Premium)
   - Selected items (cards/flowers)
   - Total amount

2. Fill in form:
   - Recipient Information (name, email, delivery date)
   - Your Information (name, email, phone)
   - Personal Message (optional)
   - Payment Method (M-Pesa or Card)

3. Click "Complete Purchase"
   - Shows confirmation
   - Returns to main shop
